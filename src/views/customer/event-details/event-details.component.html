G Three presents- Africa Day 61 years celebration with symphony culture

KICC Gardens, Nairobi Kenya

Location

36/4A, Steve Bico Auditorium,

Date & Time

25th May - 2th May 2024,
10 am - 4 pm Eastern Africa Time

About this event

The annual Africa Day event marks the founding of the Organization of African Unity, celebrating the continent's rich culture, history, and achievements. Festivities encompass cultural showcases, panel discussions, film screenings, community service projects, and youth engagement activities. Through vibrant performances, insightful dialogues, and collaborative initiatives, participants honor African heritage and promote unity, sustainability, and progress. This gathering serves as a platform for fostering connections, sharing knowledge, and inspiring positive change across Africa and beyond.

  • Cultural showcases from Africa.

  • Films by African filmmakers screened.
  • Community projects benefiting local areas.
  • Youth engagement through various activities.
  • Networking for Africa-focused collaborations.
  • Virtual events for global participation.
  • Emphasis on unity and diversity.
